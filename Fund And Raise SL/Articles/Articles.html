School Supply Donation


For children education is the best hope for breaking free poverty. In Sri Lanka some families struggle to pay for school supplies. Fund And Raise SL donated backpacks, pencils, notebooks, rulers and more to 3 schools.
The event was successful with the donators contribution and volunteers who participated in the event.
