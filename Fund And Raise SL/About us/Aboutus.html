About us

Fund and Raise
Sri-Lanka

Fund and Raise SL is a non profit organization that works with and for the rights of children and families who need help. This was started in 2022, Fund and raise SL supports the development of analytical capacity of children,families, and communities in 13 srilankan districts to assess the status of child rights. Our social workers, with the aid of our partners, start and promote women's and children's empowerment, as well as assisting families in acheiving their own socioeconomic goals. With passion, love and care ,we seek to empower individuals.
